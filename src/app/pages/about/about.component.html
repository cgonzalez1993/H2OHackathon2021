Safety tips for avoiding harmful algal blooms

Harmful Algal Blooms

Don't swim or come in direct contact with water that smells bad, looks discolored, or has scum on the surface. Don't let pets drink, play, or swim in water or eat algae. Stay out of the water if it has dead fish or other dead animals in or near it.
Algal blooms can be toxic. Keep people and pets away from water that is green, scummy or smells bad.
